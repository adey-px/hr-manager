Human Resource Portal

This Human Resource Portal (HRP) software combines a number of systems and processes to ensure the easy management of human resources, business processes and data. It is designed to be used by businesses to combine a number of necessary HR functions, such as storing employee data, managing payroll, recruitment, benefits administration, time and attendance, employee performance management, and tracking competency and training records.

The HR Portal ensures everyday human resources processes are manageable and easy to access. It merges human resources as a discipline and, in particular, its basic HR activities and processes with the information technology field. This HRP system has its origin from software that integrates information from different applications into one universal database. The linkage of its financial and human modules through one database is the most important distinction to the individually and proprietarily developed predecessors, which makes this software application both rigid and flexible.

We developers have worked to provide a means of acquiring, storing, analyzing and distributing information to various stakeholders. This Portal guarantees improvement in traditional processes and enhance strategic decision-making. The wave of technological advancement has revolutionized each and every space of life today, and this includes Human Resources management. Early systems were narrow in scope, typically focused on a single task, such as improving the payroll process or tracking employees' work hours. Today's systems cover the full spectrum of tasks associated with human resources departments, including tracking and improving process efficiency, managing organizational hierarchy, tracking absence and annual leave, simplifying financial transactions, and providing reports on people data.

This HRP is an open source project and is therefore free for use. It is avaialable as both web & desktop versions with mobile apps downloadable through the links on the Home page. Feel free to contact us in order to request a demo or a copy of the Application for your organization. The developers hope to improve and expand the software in future and hope you will find it valuable to your organization.
